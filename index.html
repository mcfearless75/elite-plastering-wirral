What We Do

Our Services

From fresh plaster to bespoke joinery and damp proofing — we handle it all with the same high standard every time.

Plastering

Flawless finishes on every wall and ceiling. Whether it's a fresh skim or full re-plaster, we use quality materials and proven techniques.

  • Skimming & full re-plaster
  • Exterior rendering
  • Patch repairs & crack filling
  • Artex removal & boarding

Joinery

Bespoke woodwork built to last. From fitted wardrobes to custom cabinets, we craft pieces that are perfectly tailored to your home.

  • Custom cabinets & wardrobes
  • Bespoke bookshelves & shelving
  • Stair banisters & handrails
  • Door frames & skirting

Media Walls

Bespoke media walls built around your vision. We design and construct stunning feature walls that combine joinery, plastering and finishing into one impressive centrepiece.

  • Fully bespoke design
  • TV & speaker integration
  • Plastered & painted finish
  • Feature lighting options

Damp Course

Stop damp before it spreads. With 15+ years in damp treatment, we diagnose the cause and apply a lasting solution — guaranteed.

  • Rising damp treatment
  • Penetrating damp surveys
  • Waterproofing & tanking
  • Condensation control
